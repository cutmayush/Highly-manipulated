## üéØ Dark Patterns Implemented

### 1. **False Urgency**
- Fake countdown timers that reset
- "Flash sale ending" banners that never actually end
- Product-level timers creating pressure

### 2. **Fake Scarcity**
- "Only 3 items left" messages
- Stock counts that randomly decrease
- "Limited" and "Last Chance" badges

### 3. **Fake Social Proof**
- Fake purchase notifications ("347 people viewing")
- Made-up customer testimonials
- False "verified buyer" reviews
- Fabricated "people bought this today" counters

### 4. **Hidden Subscriptions**
- Pre-checked newsletter checkbox with tiny text
- Misleading "claim your gift" that subscribes users
- Opt-out instead of opt-in design

### 5. **Deceptive Pricing**
- Inflated "original prices" to make discounts look huge
- Fake "80% OFF" claims
- Misleading comparative pricing

### 6. **Exit Intent Manipulation**
- Aggressive popup when trying to leave
- Countdown timer in popup creating false urgency
- Guilt-inducing "No thanks" button text

### 7. **Disguised Advertising**
- Fake awards and certifications
- "#1 Best Seller" claims without proof
- "Rated by TechReview Magazine" (non-existent)

### 8. **Forced Continuity**
- Auto-adding items to cart (extended warranty)
- Pre-selected subscription options
- Hidden recurring charges

### 9. **Confirmshaming**
- "No thanks, I prefer paying full price" button text
- Manipulative language to guilt users

### 10. **Obstruction**
- Difficult-to-close popups
- Confirmation dialogs when trying to leave
- "Are you sure you want to miss out?" messages

### 11. **Bait and Switch**
- Promises that aren't kept
- "Free gift" that requires subscription
- Discount codes that auto-apply questionable terms

### 12. **Interface Interference**
- Auto-scrolling to products
- Changing page title when user switches tabs
- Button text changing on hover to create urgency
